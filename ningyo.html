人魚が逃げた

Author:青山 美智子

Published: 2025

⭐ Review

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Since I was a kid, I love Ariel (Little Mermaid story). Though, there are many versions of ending that I known as an adult, I still love Ariel wanting to live outside her comfort zone.

In here, the mystery of the 王子様 that appears at 銀座 out of the blue and being involved in the spontaneous interview TV show, does make people wonder if he is actually a real prince(?) or he is acting as one to promote any theatrical act(?)

From multiple different stories with different characters, they show me:

(1) Being honest and truthful, with good communication between each other (as a partner) always play a good role in building the relationship

(2) can we live with each other when we have a complete opposite personalities or even hobbies? The answer is yes and no, as it depends on how you are with your partner. Once again, communication.

(3) knowing when to let go, sometimes, it’s better than holding on.
