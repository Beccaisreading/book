真相をお話しします

Author:結城真一郎

Published: 2025

⭐ Review

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

I got goosebumps reading this novel, it contains 5 short stories (contains these: grief, anger, infidelity, sincere, honesty)

The one that leave the biggest impact would be the first story, and also the last one. First story did make me think twice on what I just read through, not because of the mom, but the child 🫨

The last story reminds me more of Truman show 👀 even though I never watch the full movie, but seen the trailer and people review about it.Not all stories, but expect the unexpected for the twist on top of another twist
